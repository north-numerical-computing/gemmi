Compute the block fixed-point representation of a row/column of the matrix. This function computes the fixed-point representation of a row/column of the matrix, which is used in the splitting algorithms. It extracts the significand and sign of each element in the row/column, and stores them in the provided vectors.

Parameters
fractionVector to store the fixed-point representation of the elements.
signVector to store the signs of the elements.
iThe index of the row/column for which to compute the fixed-point representation.
